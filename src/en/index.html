ABOUT US

Our online classes replace 90% of live classes` content. You participate in a lesson directly and can ask teacher any time.

Every teacher considers series of lessons and creates a syllabus. Intensity of lessons allows to pass 1 school year in 3-4 months. Duration of 1 lesson is from 1 to 2 hours (depending on student`s skills)

Each lesson is built without actual tasks. They contain only advice on effectivity advancement.

Lessons are provided by skype, zoom, viber etc. along with demonstration of a board or screen for visual support of study material. It lessens need-be uploads of materials and changes of PC settings.
